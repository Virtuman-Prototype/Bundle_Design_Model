# Bundle_Design_Model
Bundle Co-Creation Design Model
Online Bundle Recommendation System (MVP)This project develops a data-driven bundle recommendation system prototype for JUNA Académie, focusing on the Québec wellness and fashion market. Following the Design Science Research (DSR) methodology, the system integrates text mining, topic modeling, and semantic analysis to provide personalized lifestyle experiences.🛠️ System Architecture (M1 - M4)The prototype is organized into four strategic modules, reflecting the end-to-end pipeline of the recommendation logic:M1: Strategic Brand Synergy EvaluatorObjective: Evaluate the strategic alignment between JUNA and potential partners (e.g., Rose Boréal).Logic: Uses Latent Association Analysis and Cosine Similarity to compare brand vectors across 5 dimensions: Value Congruence, Audience Overlap, Identity & Aesthetic, Functional Complementarity, and Prestige/Risk.Output: A Brand Synergy Radar Chart and a "Global Fit Index" to decide if a bundle collaboration is viable.M2: Community Insight EngineObjective: Extract consumer preferences and persona patterns from social media signals.Logic: Analyzes sentiment and recurring themes from 30,000–50,000 entries within Québec wellness communities.Output: Topic modeling trends and high-frequency "Early Adopter Hotspots" that define the current market mood.M3: Co-design LabObjective: Define the "Experience Themes" (Scenarios) and "Apparel Sets" (Content).Logic: Matches user Personas (age, interests, aesthetic) to specific Scenarios (e.g., Urban Meditation Retreat vs. Forest Yoga Immersion).Output: Structured bundle definitions that balance experiential relevance and aesthetic consistency.M4: Dynamic DeliveryObjective: Provide real-time, personalized recommendations.Logic: Based on the Liu et al. (2018) pipeline, the system adjusts recommendation lists dynamically as user interests or browsing history evolve.Output: A final "Scenario + Apparel" bundle recommendation presented through a user-friendly front-end interface.🚀 Research BackgroundMethodology: Design Science Research (DSR).Key Literature:Liu, D.-R., et al. (2018) for recommendation list adjustment.Peffers, K., et al. (2012) for DSR application in IS.Target Market: Québec early adopters in the wellness and yoga sector.
